Privacy Policy

Data Collection

Thunder Client does not intentionally collect any personal data from its users. We do not track, store, or transmit any information about your gameplay, system, or personal details.

Client Usage

When you use Thunder Client, all data remains local to your browser. The client runs entirely in your browser and does not send any information to our servers beyond the initial download of the client files.

Third-Party Servers

When connecting to Minecraft servers through Thunder Client, you are subject to the privacy policies and terms of service of those individual servers. Thunder Client is not responsible for data collected by third-party servers.

Browser Storage

Thunder Client may use your browser’s local storage to save game settings, configurations, and worlds. This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
